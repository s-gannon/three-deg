What is Three Degrees?

Three Degrees is your ultimate networking app designed to help you connect with coworkers, friends, and acquaintances through your extended network, and prove that our world is so connected that we are no longer six connections away from anyone, but maybe as few as three.

Key Features:

  • Expand Your Network: Discover connections through friends of friends.
  • Friend Finder: Easily locate and reconnect with old friends.
  • Groups and Communities: Join or create groups based on your interests and profession.
  • Privacy and Security: Your data is safe and secure with us with the option to self-host your own instance.
